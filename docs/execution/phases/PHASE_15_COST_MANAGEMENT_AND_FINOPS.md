# Phase 15 — Cost Management, Billing & AI FinOps

Version: 1.0

# Purpose

This phase defines the enterprise AI Financial Operations (FinOps) platform.

The goal is not simply to calculate costs.

The goal is to optimize profitability, efficiency, forecasting, budgeting and
resource allocation across the entire AI platform.

Every AI request generates financial data.

Every runtime generates financial data.

Every workflow generates financial data.

Everything is measurable.
